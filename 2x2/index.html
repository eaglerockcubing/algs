2x2 Algorithms

Here are all the standard algorithms we teach for solving a 2x2x2 Cube.

2x2 OLL

OLL (orientation of the last layer) is the second-to-last step in the Ortega and CFOP method. Use these algorithms to orient all the corners on the last layer.

View Algs

2x2 PBL

PBL (permutate both layers) is the last step in the Ortega method. Use these algorithms to solve the cube after orienting the top and bottom layers.
